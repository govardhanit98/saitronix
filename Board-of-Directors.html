Board Of Directors

Agni Teja, Managing Director

Kasyap Reddy has been a member of Saitronix’s board of directors since 2008, and became the Managing Director in 2014. He joined Saitronix in 2006 as Manager, Marketing and was promoted to Manager, Business Development in 2007. As Executive Director (Business Development) he was responsible for marketing Saitronix’s products internationally, interacting with customer on technical matters relating to new products, identifying product ideas for new product development, and overseeing development of new products. Mr. Reddy was also involved in various strategic activities of Saitronix. As Managing Director, he now looks after all functions of Saitronix and still spends considerable time in Product design & development. Mr. Reddy began his career with Analog Devices Inc., USA, where he worked as an Applications Engineer for three years prior to joining Saitronix. Mr. Reddy received a bachelor’s degree in electronics and communications engineering from Muffakam Jah College of Engineering and Technology, a master’s degree in electrical engineering from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, and has a master’s in business administration from Indian School of Business.

Navin Reddy, Executive Director, Marketing

Srinivas Reddy has been a member of Saitronix’s board of directors since 1998. He joined Saitronix in 1991 as a Marketing Executive and was later promoted to Marketing Manager, before joining the board of directors. He is responsible for all Marketing functions at Saitronix, like marketing of all products in India, supporting new product development and guiding marketing activities overseas. In addition to Marketing functions, Mr. Reddy enthusiastically oversees various administrative activities and is keenly involved in identifying opportunities for future growth and other strategic activities. Mr. Reddy worked for Udayam News Paper prior to joining Saitronix. Mr. Reddy received a bachelor’s degree in arts from Osmania University and a master’s degree in business administration from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University..

Vinod Agarwal, Executive Director, Production

has been a member of Saitronix’s board of directors since 2002. Mr. Agarwal joined Saitronix in 1993 as a Production Engineer. He was promoted to Production Manager in 1995 and to General Manager, Production in 1999 before joining the board of directors. Mr. Agarwal has given a number of fruitful years of his career to Saitronix and he is currently responsible for production and quality control of all products at Saitronix. Mr. Agarwal also serves as a management representative for ISO 9001:2008 quality system. His expertise lies in electronics industry in the area of production methods and practices. Mr. Agarwal worked for Consys Electronics as Project Engineer for three years prior to joining Saitronix. He received a bachelor’s degree in electrical and electronics engineering from Nagpur University

Mayank, Executive Director, Control Electronics

Subodh Yeotikar has been a member of Saitronix’s board of directors since 2002. Mr. Yeotikar joined Medha in 1999 as Assistant General Manager, Control Electronics. He is a pioneer in developing products for locomotive control, speed and energy monitoring systems. Currently, he oversees overall system level research, design, complete product development and field failure analysis. Mr. Yeotikar began his career with Hindustan Brown Boveri as Research and Development Engineer, where he worked for three years. Over the years, he has gained more than 20 years of experience in product research & development and control & monitoring systems for railways and automobiles. Mr. Yeotikar received a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from Government Engineering College and a master’s degree in electrical engineering from Indian Institute of Technology.
